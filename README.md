# 🛍️ Customer Behavior Analysis Dashboard

A comprehensive **Customer Behavior Analysis** project built using **Python** **SQL** and **Power BI** to clean, analyze shopping trends, customer demographics, seasonal sales, product performance, and purchasing behavior.

The project transforms raw customer transaction data into actionable business insights through data cleaning, SQL analysis, and interactive Power BI visualizations.

---

## 📌 Project Overview

The objective of this project is to understand customer purchasing patterns and identify business opportunities using data analytics techniques.

The dashboard helps answer questions such as:

- Which season generates the highest revenue?
- Which product category performs the best?
- Which customer segment contributes the most sales?
- Which states generate the highest revenue?
- How effective is the subscription program?
- What business actions can improve sales?

## 🔍 Key Insights

- 🍂 Fall generated the highest sales revenue.
- ☀️ Summer recorded the lowest sales.
- 👕 Clothing is the highest revenue-generating category.
- 👨 Male customers account for approximately **68%** of total customers.
- 👩 Female customers account for **32%** of customers.
- 📍 Montana generated the highest revenue.
- 👥 Young Adults represent the largest customer segment.
- 💳 Payment methods are evenly distributed.
- ⭐ The average customer rating is **3.75**, indicating opportunities to improve customer satisfaction.
- 💼 Non-subscribed customers contribute more revenue than subscribed customers.

## 💡 Business Recommendations

### 📉 Increase Summer Sales

Launch **Summer-exclusive promotions** featuring **Clothing + Footwear combo offers** to improve footwear sales and increase overall seasonal revenue.

---

### 👩 Increase Female Customer Engagement

Introduce targeted marketing campaigns, personalized recommendations, and exclusive discounts to attract more female customers.

---

### 👟 Improve Lower Performing Categories

Bundle Clothing with Footwear or Outerwear to encourage cross-selling and increase average order value.

---

### ⭐ Improve Customer Satisfaction

Analyze customer reviews to identify the primary causes of lower ratings, focusing on:

- Product Quality
- Pricing
- Shipping Experience
- Customer Support

---

### 🎁 Improve Subscription Program

Offer exclusive discounts, loyalty rewards, free shipping, and early product access to increase subscription adoption.

---

### 📍 Expand Successful Regional Strategies

Replicate successful sales and marketing strategies from Montana in lower-performing regions.
